eDrive Application Info

eDrive is your intelligent mobile application designed

to assist you with your journey planning in Kuala Lumpur

areas via effective two way communication between you

and ITIS DBKL.


eDrive disseminates traffic congestion and incidents information

collected to keep the road users informed and alert.

On top of that, eDrive’s two way communication utilizes text

and image for convenience. This allows you to report traffic

related cases for rectification and action.


Other features also includes parking space information,

traffic CCTV views, VMS Message Boards views and DBKL

Engagements information.


Disclaimer: eDrive’s coverage is only restricted to

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
